Neurospicy Agenda Reminder — What it does

A Google Workspace™ add-on (for Google Sheets™ & Google Calendar™) that emails you a weekly agenda digest. You choose which calendars to include, on which day(s) and time to send, and you can optionally attach two list tabs from a Sheet (Boodschappen and Klusjes). Emails are sent via Gmail™.

How it works

  1. Install the add-on in Google Sheets™ and open the sidebar.
  2. Select your calendars, pick day(s) and time (e.g., Mon & Fri at 08:00).
  3. Optionally include the two list tabs from your Sheet.
  4. The add-on creates time-based triggers and sends the digest at your chosen times.

Permissions (OAuth scopes) used

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly — read events to build the weekly digest.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly — read list tabs in the active spreadsheet only.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp — create/remove time-based triggers.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.send_mail — send the digest email.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email — bind settings to your account’s primary email.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.locale — use your locale for date/time formatting.
