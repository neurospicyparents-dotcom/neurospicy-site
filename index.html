Neurospicy Agenda Reminder — What it does

A Google Workspace Add-on (for Google Sheets & Calendar) that emails you a weekly agenda digest. You choose which calendars to include, on which day(s) and time to send, and you can optionally attach two list tabs from a Sheet (Boodschappen and Klusjes).

How it works

  1. Install the add-on in Google Sheets and open the sidebar.
  2. Select your calendars, pick day(s) and time (e.g., Mon & Fri at 08:00).
  3. Optionally include the two list tabs from your Sheet.
  4. The add-on creates time-based triggers and sends the digest at your chosen times.

Permissions (OAuth scopes) used

  • calendar.readonly — read events to build the weekly digest.
  • spreadsheets.readonly — read the two optional list tabs.
  • spreadsheets.currentonly — read spreadsheets only where the add-on is installed.
  • script.scriptapp — create and remove time-based triggers you control.
  • script.send_mail — send the digest email to your specified recipients.
  • userinfo.email — tie settings to your Google account’s primary email.
  • script.locale — use your locale for date/time formatting.
